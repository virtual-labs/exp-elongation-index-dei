### Procedure

### Step 1: Weighing Coarse Aggregate Sample
•	Click on the tray to place it on the weighing balance.
<img src="procedure_images/1.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click the "TARE" button to zero the balance .
<img src="procedure_images/1.2.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click on the coarse aggregate sample to measure its weight . 
<img src="procedure_images/1.3.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click the "Next Step" button.

### Step 2: Preparation for Sieve Analysis of Coarse Aggregates
•	Click on the Assemble button to assemble sieves in descending order.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/2.1.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click on the aggregate sample to pour into the sieve stack . 
<img src="procedure_images/2.2.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click on the lid to cover the sieve stack 
<img src="procedure_images/2.3.png" alt="procedure" width="300"> <br>    
•	Click on the sieve shaker to place it on the sieve shaker.
<img src="procedure_images/2.4.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click on the Tare button to start shaking. 
<img src="procedure_images/2.5.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click on the lid to remove sieve from the shaker. 
<img src="procedure_images/2.6.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click on the sieve and note down the aggregates.
<img src="procedure_images/2.7.png" alt="procedure" width="300"><br>
•	Click the "Next Step" button.
